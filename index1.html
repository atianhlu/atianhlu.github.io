WHO AM I: EPISTEMIC LOGIC IN FIGHT CLUB

Huaitian Lu (S3339246), Bettina Soos (S3421554), Xingchi Su (P286498)

`

Introduction

This project is to explore the epistemic logic in the movie Fight Club. The film tells a story of a man, Jack, with schizophrenia who finally identifies his another totally different personality, Tyler. We especially modeled the final flashback scene how the truth was revealed in Jack's mind. Alongside the story line, Jack would eliminate the worlds where he and Tyler obey distinguished predicates step by step. Consequently, the remaining possible worlds in the end are the worlds where Jack and Tyler share the absolutely same predicates. The logic ELA (Epistemic Logic with Assignment) will be applied in our case with some modification.

Agents

Jack
Lives in a dreary life as an office worker, full of crisis for things around him, even joined the healing groups of different diseases to cry out his severe insomnia.
Tyler
A businessman who sales soap. Tyler is rebellious, tough and violent, he believes that a person shouldn't be defined by consumerism and should do whatever he wants.
Marla
Knowing Jack firstly, then started to see Tyler oftenly. She helped Jack to reveal the truth that he is Tyler.

Previous story

  • Jack met Tyler on a plane. After the flight, Jack found his own department was on fire, then he started to live together with Tyler in his dilapidated house. The two became close friends and created the "Fight Club": an underground organization that allows people to fight like a savage to vent their emotions.
  • Besides the fighting, crazy group activities were also included, such as stealing human fat from hospitals to make soap and causing damage to the infrastructure system of the city as a prank.
  • Gradually, more and more blind believers joined the club, it became a national underground organization, and the activites became more dangerours and seemingly out of control.
  • Jack becomes intolerable about the current situation of "Fight Club" and Tyler's behavior, they had a dispute, and Tyler left. However, Jack found he just couldn't get rid of Tyler's shadow wherever he went. He began to think: Who am I?
